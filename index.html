Does Oscar Health Cover Mental Health?

Yes. Every Oscar Health plan in New York covers mental health and substance use treatment as essential health benefits, including outpatient psychotherapy, psychiatric evaluations, and virtual behavioral health visits at the same cost-sharing level as medical care.

Coverage for therapy varies from one Oscar plan to another. Most plans have a fixed copay per session, with the deductible waived for behavioral health. Some plans do not waive the deductible. With an MMHC Oscar therapist, your therapy sessions will likely cost between $0 and $25 per session. The $0–$25 range is the copay (the fixed amount you owe at each visit).

For most Oscar Gold and Platinum members, the deductible is waived for psychotherapy. If that is the case, you will be responsible for any applicable Oscar copay.

Why Finding an In-Network Oscar Therapist in NYC Is Harder Than It Should Be

Understanding your Oscar benefits is one thing. Actually using them is harder, and the data explains why.

According to the HRSA State of the Behavioral Health Workforce (2025), the national average wait time for a behavioral health appointment is 48 days, and 6 in 10 psychologists are not accepting new patients. New York City faces some of the most acute supply constraints in the country due to population density, sustained post-COVID demand, and high provider attrition across the behavioral health sector.

That gap has structural roots. The NYC Council’s Mental Health Roadmap documented 30 to 40% vacancy rates across the city’s behavioral health sector following COVID-19. The NYC Department of Health & Mental Hygiene’s first-ever comprehensive mental health report (2024) found that 18% of adult New Yorkers have been diagnosed with anxiety and 14% reported serious psychological distress as recently as 2022, demand that far outstrips available in-network supply.

This is precisely the problem MMHC is built to solve. Rather than directing you to a directory to search alone, we match you directly with a licensed clinician and confirm your Oscar coverage before your first session.

The Research: Why Therapist Match Quality Matters More for Oscar Members in Virtual Therapy

The Seamless Rematch Policy isn’t a marketing line. It’s a response to specific clinical research on why virtual therapy makes the right initial match more important.

A landmark meta-analysis of 295 studies covering more than 30,000 patients (Flückiger et al., 2018) found that therapeutic alliance is one of the most consistent predictors of treatment success across all modalities, countries, and patient populations.

In telehealth specifically, a 2024 systematic review and meta-analysis in Clinical Psychology Review (Aafjes-van Doorn et al.) found that while therapeutic alliance predicts outcomes in virtual therapy, the effect is somewhat weaker than in person, suggesting that getting the right initial match is even more important when therapy is delivered online. ACORN Collaboration data confirms that therapist characteristics, not patient characteristics, account for the largest share of alliance quality variance. Which therapist you see matters more than who you are as a patient.

For Oscar members navigating the Optum directory alone, the research argues for a supported matching process with a no-friction rematch option—exactly what MMHC provides.

Frequently Asked Questions: Oscar Health Insurance and Therapy in NYC

Is Manhattan Mental Health Counseling in-network with Oscar Health Insurance?+

Yes. MMHC is an in-network behavioral health provider for Oscar Health Insurance in New York, participating through the Optum Behavioral Health network that administers Oscar’s mental health benefits in New York, California, and Texas.

Does Oscar cover virtual therapy (telehealth) in New York?+

Yes. Oscar Health Insurance covers virtual mental health counseling for New York members, typically at the same copay as in-person sessions. The New York Health Foundation (2024) confirms that psychological and psychiatric telehealth is the most utilized telehealth service category in New York State.

What is the average copay for therapy with Oscar Health?+

Most Oscar members pay between $0 and $25 per therapy session. Oscar Simple Gold typically has a flat $10 copay. Oscar Simple Secure offers the first three sessions at $0 for eligible members. Gold and Platinum plans generally range from $10 to $25 per session with the deductible waived for behavioral health. Bronze and Silver plans may require coinsurance after the deductible is met.

Does Oscar require a referral for therapy?+

No. Oscar Health Insurance does not require a referral from a primary care physician for outpatient mental health therapy. You can self-refer directly to an in-network provider such as MMHC and schedule your first session.

Is there a session limit on therapy with Oscar?+

Oscar Health plans do not impose a preset annual session limit on outpatient psychotherapy. You can attend therapy on a weekly basis as long as your Oscar coverage is active and there is a medically necessary reason for your visits, consistent with the federal Mental Health Parity and Addiction Equity Act.

How do I know if my Oscar plan covers EMDR or IFS therapy?+

EMDR and IFS are recognized evidence-based psychotherapy modalities billed under standard behavioral health CPT codes (90834 and 90837). If your Oscar plan covers outpatient psychotherapy, it covers EMDR and IFS at the same copay. You can verify by calling the Oscar Member Care Team at 1-855-672-2755 or signing in at hioscar.com.

What is the Optum Behavioral Health network, and why does it matter for Oscar members?+

Optum Behavioral Health administers Oscar’s mental health and substance use benefits in New York. When you search for an in-network therapist through Oscar, you are searching the Optum provider directory. MMHC is a participating Optum provider, which means all sessions are billed at in-network rates under your Oscar behavioral health benefit.

Can I switch therapists at MMHC without restarting the intake process?+

Yes. MMHC’s Seamless Rematch Policy allows you to switch to a different MMHC clinician without re-verification, a new intake, or lost progress. If after your first or second session you feel the fit is not right, we match you with another therapist at no additional friction.

How quickly can I start therapy at MMHC with Oscar insurance?+

Most Oscar members complete their pre-screening within a few days and begin therapy the same week. The NYS Department of Financial Services mandates that insurers ensure timely behavioral health access within 10 business days. Booking directly with MMHC is the most reliable path to meeting that window.
